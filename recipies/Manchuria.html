Veg Manchurian

Description

Cabbage is usually the leading vegetable in the
manchurian balls, with carrots, green beans, bell
eppers and even cauliflower added to the mix. I use
cabbage, carrots and green bell peppers, which
gives me the best results, and my recipe is
tailored to these veggies.

To prepare the vegetable balls: Take all the veggies, finely chopped, in a bowl along
with ginger, garlic, corn flour, maida, salt, black
pepper and soy sauce. 2. Mix them well, adding water
as required for consistency.
