Biryani

Description

Biryani is most favorite food of people in Hyderabad. They prefer eating Chicken Dum Biryani in restaurants. There are some of the famous restaurants that serve this dish with a special taste and one among them is Paradise biryani.

The cost of Biryani is around 150 Rs which serves atleast two people in a pack. This is a stomach full of eating and tastes very spicy as it is a spicy food of telangana in special occasions

List of Ingredients

  • Basmati Long Rice
  • Biryani leaf
  • chicken
  • Garam Masala
  • Badam
  • Water

Step by Step Making of dish

  1. Boil the water and cook the basmati rice partially
  2. Marinate the chicken with curd and masalas
  3. cut the onions and deep fry the onions to brown onions
  4. arrange layers of rice, onions and chicken
