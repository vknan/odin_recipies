Chapathi

Description

Chapathi is made with wheat flour. It is soft and delicious when fried on pan

Chapati, also known as roti, rotli, safati, shabaati, phulka, chapo, and roshi,
is an unleavened flatbread originating from the Indian subcontinent and staple
in India, Nepal, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, East Africa, Arabian
Peninsula and the Caribbean.

  • wheat Flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Step by step making of dish

  1. mix water with wheat flour and add pinch of salt.
  2. make chapathis with chapathi roller
  3. heat the pan and make chapathis
